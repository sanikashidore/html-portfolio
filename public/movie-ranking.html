The Best Movies According to Sanika

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Lighting up the Stars (2022)

"Lighting Up the Stars" is a heartwarming Chinese drama that tackles the heavy themes of loss, grief, and healing in a surprisingly light and moving way. It follows the story of a former convict who crosses paths with a little girl and unexpectedly finds purpose in life through their unlikely bond. The film blends humor, tenderness, and raw emotion, supported by strong performances from the lead actors. It’s a story about rediscovery and redemption that leaves you thinking about the power of connection long after the credits roll.


Spirited Away (2001)

Hayao Miyazaki’s "Spirited Away" is a mesmerizing animated masterpiece that transports viewers to a magical world full of strange creatures and deep, metaphorical layers. The story of young Chihiro, who finds herself in a mysterious spirit realm while trying to save her parents, is as enchanting as it is profound. With breathtaking animation, unforgettable characters, and a narrative that blends whimsy with darker, introspective themes, "Spirited Away" is a timeless tale about growing up, courage, and the beauty of self-discovery.


Pretty Woman (1990)

"Pretty Woman" is a charming romantic comedy that pairs Julia Roberts and Richard Gere in one of the most iconic love stories of the '90s. The film tells the unlikely Cinderella-esque tale of a wealthy businessman and a down-on-her-luck escort who form an unexpected connection. Julia Roberts’ radiant performance as Vivian brings warmth and charisma to the role, while Richard Gere’s suave demeanor provides a perfect balance. With its memorable moments, catchy soundtrack, and fairytale charm, "Pretty Woman" is a feel-good classic that still resonates with audiences today.
